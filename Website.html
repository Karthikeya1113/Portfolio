About Me

I am a Farm Machinery R&D Engineer passionate about designing and developing innovative agricultural equipment that bridges the gap between traditional farming practices and modern mechanization needs. With a strong background in agricultural engineering, machine design, and sustainable farming systems, I specialize in creating solutions that enhance productivity, efficiency, and farmer accessibility.

My core interests lie in:

  • Design & Development of advanced farm implements and machinery.
  • Prototyping & Testing of mechanized solutions for real-world field conditions.
  • Automation & Smart Technologies in agriculture (IoT, sensors, AI integration).
  • Sustainable Mechanization that reduces labor, fuel, and environmental impact.
